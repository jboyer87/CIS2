Project Workshop

Tonight we will be sharing our project ideas and holding a project workshop. Please take a few minutes to prepare a quick presentation on your project answering the following questions:

  1. What is the topic/purpose of the webpage you are building?
  2. Who is the target audience?
  3. How many pages do you anticipate your page will have?
  4. What are some of the pages that you will need to build?
  5. Have you looked at any websites for inspiration (style or content)?
